Tribute to Neil Armstrong

Neil Armstrong was an American astronaut and aeronautical engineer who made history as the first person to walk on the Moon. Born on August 5, 1930, in Wapakoneta, Ohio, Armstrong developed a passion for aviation early in life. He earned his pilot's license at the age of 16 and later pursued a degree in aeronautical engineering from Purdue University.

  • Born: August 5, 1930
  • Died: August 25, 2012
  • Occupation: Astronaut and Aeronautical Engineer
  • Known for: First person to walk on the Moon
